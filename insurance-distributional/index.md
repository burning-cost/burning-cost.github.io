Distributional gradient boosting for insurance pricing. Models the full conditional distribution of claims, not just the mean. Includes TweedieGBM, GammaGBM, ZIPGBM, and NegBinomialGBM with proper offset handling for exposure. Double GLM implementation (Smyth-Jorgensen) for dispersion modelling and per-risk volatility scoring.
